What is Kombucha?

Kombucha is a fizzy, fermented drink full of health benefits and has been around for thousands of years. Originating in ancient China, kombucha is sweetened tea that is fermented with the help of beneficial yeasts and bacteria. This great tasting drink is a good way to make to add beneficial probiotics to your diet. In addition to keeping your digestion healthy the bacteria also produce beneficial acids during the fermentation process that can help with your overall health and longevity.
